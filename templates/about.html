Our Story

Bringing Color to Your Ideas

Founded in 2018, Print Village started as a small printing shop in Kathmandu with one HP Indigo press and a big dream — to make premium quality printing accessible to everyone. Today, we serve thousands of customers across Nepal with state-of-the-art technology and unmatched customer service.
